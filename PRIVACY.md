# Privacy Policy — Budgy

Budgy is a personal finance management application for strictly personal use, part of the CustHome personal portal.

## Data collected
Budgy retrieves account information (balances, account details) and transactions from the owner's own bank accounts, through PSD2 Account Information Services (AIS) provided by Enable Banking. No other personal data is collected.

## Data usage
The data is used exclusively to provide budgeting features to the owner: transaction categorization, budget tracking, recurring payment detection and spending dashboards.

## Storage and protection
All bank data is stored in the owner's own infrastructure and encrypted at rest. It is never shared with, sold to, or made accessible to any third party.

## Data deletion
The owner can delete linked accounts, transactions and consents at any time, which permanently removes the associated data.
